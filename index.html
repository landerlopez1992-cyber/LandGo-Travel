About LandGo Travel

LandGo Travel is a mobile application development platform specializing in travel booking technology solutions. We provide comprehensive travel reservation services through our integrated mobile and web applications.

Our platform connects users with official travel data providers to deliver real-time booking information, competitive pricing, and seamless reservation experiences across multiple travel services.

Important Compliance Notice: LandGo Travel strictly complies with all U.S. sanctions and international regulations. We do not provide services to or accept bookings for destinations in jurisdictions subject to sanctions, including Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, and the Crimea, Donetsk, and Luhansk regions.

  • Technology platform for travel booking services
  • Integrated with authorized travel data providers (Duffel API)
  • Secure payment processing with Stripe
  • Mobile-first design and development
  • Full compliance with U.S. sanctions and international regulations
  • Based in Florida, United States

Our Services

We specialize in developing and maintaining travel booking applications that serve customers worldwide. Our technology stack includes:

  • Flutter-based mobile applications
  • Supabase backend infrastructure
  • Stripe payment processing integration
  • Duffel API for flight and hotel data
  • Real-time booking and reservation management
  • Customer relationship management systems
